📖 How to Play

🔵

Source & Sink

Every puzzle has a Source node (blue — water enters the system) and a Sink node (teal — water exits). Your goal is to push as much water as possible from Source to Sink.

🎚️

Adjust the Sliders

Each pipe has a capacity (the maximum water it can carry) and a flow (how much you actually send). Drag the slider on a pipe to set its flow — anywhere from 0 up to its capacity.

⚖️

Conservation Rule

At every middle node, water flowing in must equal water flowing out. You can't store water mid-network! The game highlights any node that's out of balance in red.

🏆

Find the Maximum

Once the network is balanced, hit Check Flow. You win the level when your total flow equals the network's true maximum — which you'll have to discover!

Key insight: there's always a bottleneck somewhere in the network. The maximum flow is limited by the smallest total capacity across any "cut" of pipes that separates Source from Sink. Finding that cut is the secret!
